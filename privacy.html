NumNum Workout – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

When you connect your Whoop account, we access your recovery score, strain score, and sleep data through the Whoop API. This data is used solely to display your metrics within the NumNum Workout app.

How we use your data

Your Whoop data is displayed only to you inside the app. We do not share, sell, or transmit your health data to any third parties. Whoop API tokens are stored locally on the server you run and are never sent elsewhere.

Data storage

All data is stored locally on your own device. NumNum Workout does not use cloud databases or external analytics services. Your workout progress and Whoop tokens remain on your machine.

Third-party services

The app connects to the Whoop API (api.prod.whoop.com) to retrieve your health metrics. Whoop's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data on their platform.

Your rights

You can disconnect your Whoop account at any time from within the app, which deletes your stored tokens. You can also delete the local token file (whoop_tokens.json) to remove all stored credentials.
