NumNum Workout – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

NumNum Workout ("the App") is a personal workout coaching application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Information we collect

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your name and login credentials. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

Workout data: The App records your exercise logs, set completions, weights, reps, and personal notes. This data is used to track your progress and allow your coach to monitor your training.

Device data: We store certain preferences (theme, exercise customisations, and session data) locally on your device using browser storage. This data stays on your device.

Health integrations: If you connect your Whoop account, we access your recovery score, strain score, and sleep data through the Whoop API. This data is used solely to display your metrics within the App.

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the App's functionality: displaying your workout programs, tracking your progress, and enabling your coach to review and adjust your training. We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to your fitness coaching.

Data sharing

Your workout data may be visible to your assigned coach through the admin dashboard. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Authentication uses industry-standard JWT tokens transmitted over HTTPS. All communication between the App and our servers is encrypted. Local data on your device is stored in your browser's local storage.

Third-party services

The App may connect to the Whoop API (api.prod.whoop.com) if you choose to link your Whoop account. Whoop's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data on their platform. Google Fonts is used for typography.

Data retention

Your workout data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can disconnect your Whoop account at any time from within the App. To request data deletion or export, contact us at the email below.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
